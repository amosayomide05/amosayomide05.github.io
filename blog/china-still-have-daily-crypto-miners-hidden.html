China Still have unknown crypto mining company





Crypto currency was banned in china on September 2021, but some mining companies didn't take heed to the instruction as they are still operating in unknown locations in China

CNBC have been informed by multiple sources that 20% of cryptocurrency miners are from China. According to Qihoo 360, average of 109,000 IP addresses from China are found still mining on a daily basis. Alot of these IP address are located to provinces of Jiangsu, Zhejiang, Guangdong and Shandong.
