Liverpool Pre-Match Conference

8th January 2021



Krawietz: "A team that is well-managed, you can see that. They will have targets, our situation is not ideal."

Krawietz on Covid cases:
"A few covid cases with U23s, some are back now and we can choose a team that is able to follow our football way. We want to play a proper game tomorrow, compact, aggressive, attacking, playing good football." #awlive [lfc]

Krawietz on giving younger players a chance tomorrow:
"I hope that excitement is the right word for that. I hope they can't wait till the game starts and we try to make them as confident as possible before. We try to prepare them as much as possible." #awlive [lfc]

Krawietz on Leighton Clarkson and the team tomorrow:
"We have one loan player back and he will probably join the squad tomorrow. We found enough players to have a squad for tomorrow. It will be an exciting team." #awlive [lfc]

Krawietz on training in the last week:
"Training grounds closed and shut down means nobody can come here of course. We tried to stay in contact with our players during this time like we did during the lockdown period." #awlive [lfc]

Krawietz: "Thanks to our really hard working athlete department we were able to find solutions for our players who were allowed to go out. They could go for runs outside. They had individual training programmes to keep them in shape."

Krawietz on Firmino, Alisson and Matip:
"I can't talk about names [but] Joel Matip joined in training yesterday but we will have to see tomorrow whether he is available or not." #awlive [lfc]

Peter Krawietz has just finished speaking to the media ahead of Liverpool vs Shrewsbury Town on Sunday. 🔴

Virgil van Dijk on Pep Lijnders:
"I was really happy for Pep that he was given full responsibility from the boss [against Chelsea]. I also personally told him that it is nice that the boss has such great confidence in him." #awlive [de telegraaf]

Van Dijk: “Unfortunately we didn't beat Chelsea, but it was nice to see how the preparation went and how Pep coached during the game. You suddenly get a lot of responsibility as an assistant, but you have to know that he certainly has the respect of the players at Liverpool.”

Virgil van Dijk:
"I have set the bar high myself. When you're on the pitch, everyone thinks you're 100 per cent and I understand that. You've won all those awards and so that's become the standard." #awlive [de telegraaf

Van Dijk: “I do my very best at this club and try to play the best match I can play every week. There are certainly times when it could have been better. But I am human and everyone makes mistakes.”

Virgil van Dijk on Manchester City:
“City got through the month of December particularly well. They had few injuries and few corona cases and that helps enormously." #awlive [de telegraaf]

Van Dijk: “A small example is that me, Fabinho and Thiago all tested positive for covid at the same time, three players who are important to the team. A big club like Liverpool still has good quality players, but the rhythm and dynamics of the team changes at such a time.”
