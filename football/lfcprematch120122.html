Liverpool Pre-Match Conference against Arsenal Carabao Cup

12th January 2021




Klopp on Mo Salah's new contract:

"Good conversations - that is all I can say." #awlive [lfc]

Klopp on injuries:
"Trent trained yesterday - Thiago and Divock Origi did not. All the others [as long as they don't test positive] are available apart from the ones in Africa." #awlive [lfc]

Klopp:
"Robbo got a little knock in the Shrewsbury game, but he is fine." #awlive [lfc]

Klopp on Harvey Elliott:
"Harvey Elliott looks really promising on the pitch, he is not too far away from team training." #awlive [lfc]

Klopp on Mo Salah:
"Things take time - there are so many things you have to do in these negotiations. The agent is there as well, but there is nothing to worry about. This is normal. It is all fine, it is not done but there are talks - he is a world-class player." #awlive [lfc]

Klopp: "He is a world-class boy. Of course we want to keep him. Now let's see how it will work out. That's all."

Klopp on Kaide Gordon:
"Kaide is always in our minds because he is an exceptional talent. He had a little drop in form which is completely normal for his age. He is an outstanding talent, he is always in my mind as long as he is fit. We will see where that leads." #awlive [lfc]

Klopp: "We won it with 6 Academy lads involved, so that was really big for us. It's really nice, I felt really good after the game because of so many nice stories. Step by step, that is how football education works."

Klopp on Arsenal:
"They are in a really good place. Mikel is doing an excellent job there. It's really exciting to watch - they have a good setup and a clear idea so I think they have improved a lot between the last time we played them. A tricky draw." #awlive [lfc]

Klopp on home advantage:
"With the power Anfield can produce, it will make a difference playing away from home in the second leg. We couldn't play last Thursday so it is how it is." #awlive [lfc]

Klopp on Liverpool's 'false positives' last week:

"A false positive is a positive test - but when you do a re-test, you get a negative result, so it looks like a false positive. So then you take a third test - but you cannot use the players. They are the rules." #awlive [lfc]

Klopp: "When we got the first results, we had to consider that it was the right result. We couldn't do anything else."

Klopp also confirmed that Alisson Becker trained yesterday.

Curtis Jones:
"A goal for the season was to get into the team, play, goals, assists and to help the team. I was doing that well and then it was the day before a huge game at Anfield against Atletico Madrid in the Champions League and I got poked in my eye." #awlive [lfc]

Jones: "From then, I'm out for six-and-a-half weeks. I came back, trained twice and then COVID. So it's been a hard two, three months but I'm back now, I'm working hard and I'm excited."
